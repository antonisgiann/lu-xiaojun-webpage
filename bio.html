Biography

Lu Xiaojun is a Chinese weightlifter.He has 1.72 meters height.Currently he has weight 80.41kg and he is compteting in the 81kg class.He was born in Hubei Province a village administrated by Qianjiang City,in 27 July 1984.His father and the rest of his family were farmers.When he was 13 years old he joined a local sports school to be trained as a weightlifter.In 1999 he was sent by coach Dengling Hu to join the provincial team.In 2003 Lu Xiaojun represented the Hubei in the national weightlifting championships in the 69kg class and he won the bronze medal.The same year he joined the Chinese national team.In 2006 he left the team because of injuries in his shoulders and legs.In 2008 after the 2008 summer Olympics Lu Xiaojun return to the chinese team with coach Yu Jie.Lu won the gold medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the 77kg class with a total of 379kg.His snatch of 175kg set both a world and Olympic record,as did his total 379kg,for the 77kg class.Lu missed his attempt at 177kg due to an error during the snatch phase.Lu has won 3 gold medals in the 77kg class at the 2013 world weightlifting championships.He broke his own world record in the snatch with a lift of 176kg.He also completed a 204kg clean and jerk setting a new record total of 380kg.Lu won 4 world weightlifting championships,three in the 77kg class and on ine the 81kg class.He is currently competing in the newly formated weight class of 81kg.He won the gold in the first world weightlifting championship of the 81kg class with a total of 374kg setting a new total world record for the 81kg class.Lu is well known for his amazing snatch technique considered the snatch king of his class.Also another characteristic of his lifting technique is his deep squat jerk.
Lu Xiaojun married his long-term girlfriend Guo Xiyan,a former world class weightlifter herself, in December 2013.They have one child together born in August 2014.
